Tea is a drink of health & beauty for you

Tea is rich in polyphenols that have effects like reducing inflammation and helping to fight cancer. Green tea is about 30 percent polyphenols by weight, including large amounts of a catechin called EGCG. Catechins are natural antioxidants that help prevent cell damage and provide other benefits.

Black Tea

Black tea are entirely fermented giving a stronger, full- bodied flavor and a dark-brown infusion.

Green Tea

Green tea contains antioxidants that may lower blood sugar, prevent cancer, and help with weight loss.

Herbal Tea

Herbal tea is an infusion of various leaves, fruits, bark,flowers belonging to almost any edible, non-tea plant.

Orange Tea

Orange pekoe is a grade of black tea brewed from the dried leaves and buds of the Camellia sinensis plant.
